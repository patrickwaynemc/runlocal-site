2027 Local Elections in Indiana

2027 is an odd-numbered year, when many U.S. cities, counties, school districts, and special districts hold their regular local elections (this varies by state and municipality). Offices commonly up for election in 2027 include:

  • City / town council seats
  • Mayoral races (in cities with odd-year cycles)
  • County commission / board of supervisors seats
  • School board seats
  • Zoning board / planning commission seats (appointed in many jurisdictions, elected in some)

Exact 2027 election dates and which specific seats are on the ballot in Indiana are set locally and published closer to the election. Check your county/city clerk's office and the official sources below for the confirmed 2027 local election calendar as it's released.
